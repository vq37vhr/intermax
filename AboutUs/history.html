 History




●2001 Company established in Hong Kong, Shanghai and initial service from marine spares logistics.

●2002 Acquired certificates of harbor business entry, Class A approved Forwarder, Import-Export trading license.

●2003 Joined CIFA membership, set up Ningbo office.

●2006 formed our own truck team, rent Warehouse in Shanghai Waigaoqiao.

●2007 Acquired NVOCC MOC-NV3006, FMC Bond-KMS52005, set up Qingdao office.

●2008 Joined WCAPN and IMPA membership, formed Project Team focusing on oversize cargo logistics solution, set up Shenzhen office.

●2009 set up Zhangjiagang office to better coordinate/control north south bound traffic inland transportation overspread to Jiangnan ship building base.

●2010 set up QingDao, DaLian warehouse, and MAG Selection Co., Ltd serving imp/exp purposes; bought in 2 X 17.5m low flatbed truck, and 1 X 13m heavy load truck for oversize & heavy cargo transportation; Joined GPLN, CEE, CIFA as membership.

●2011 InterMax head office moved to Shanghai property owned building; InterMax Guangzhou and Beijing office established.

●2012 Wuxi Branch office set up and subsidiary “Speedy Dragon” bought in series of 6.2 meters carriage truck; InterMax whole offices update internet hardware and software, improve server capability and mail system 263.net; facilitated with P2FMS, TMS, WMS, GPS and OA system.

●2012 Chongqing Branch was established to serve our customers better and boost the development of the southwest region for future project business.

●2013 Chengdu Branch was established to have further development of the Southwest region of China.KLG-ITM Logistics Solution Ltd was established as a joint venture with a European company , the KLG Europe Group. It serves quality warehouse service for international retailer in Shanghai Yangshan Port. InterMax has accquired a Yangtze River shipping company ie. "Hualong Shipping Co." . The company was reorganized and named "Max Shipping Co. Ltd." which owns 6 x 1000-1800tons project cargo feeder. Deyang and Leshan branches were set up.

●2014 Zhangjiagang Warehouse expanded its warehouse service and facilities and introduced advanced warehouse management system, as well as barcode scanning and storage of goods in professional storage management.

●2015 Kaohsiung company was set up to provide more professional project logistics services in Taiwan and connecting the world.

●2016 Taipei company was set up to serve Taiwan clients and as a service base to provide global logistics services. The growing of ASEAN cooperation and treaties signed among South East Asia countries were getting more important. Malaysia office was our first step in expanding our service in ASEAN countries and set up logistics platform.
