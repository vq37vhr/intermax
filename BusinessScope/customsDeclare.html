 Customs Declaration



InterMax Logistics Solution Ltd. was established in 2009 as Subsidiary branch in China with a registered capital of RMB 5 million, With the advantages of import and export, international logistics and international trade, InterMax has accumulated rich experience import and export by sea, air and rail.

InterMax covers Dalian, Tianjin, Qingdao, Shanghai, Ningbo, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Hong Kong, Taiwan, Malaysia, Thailand and other major ports which fastens the speed to complete these ports of the sea, air import and export declarations.

With the development of "one belt one road", InterMax has set up branches in Chongqing, Chengdu, Wuhan, Zhengzhou, Lianyungang which are the major starting points for Central Asian railway and handle all kinds of railway import and export declarations.
