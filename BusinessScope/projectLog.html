 Project Logistics



Project logistics

InterMax is one of the leading service providers of EPC project logistics and heavy lift logistics. Turnkey project solutions including tailor made project logistics solution, logistics schedule, transport and technical problem solving, risk management, communication among parties, and supervision of operation can be provided according to client’s needs.

Services:

- Project logistics solutions design and consultation service
- Monitoring and managing suppliers
- Implementation of multimodal logistics solutions (sea, air , rail , road, domestic barge etc.)
- Site and routes investigation in advance
- Supervision of cargo handling at port
- Transport packaging consultation, guidance of special tools and tooling
- Heavy Logistics
- Unloading at construction site
- Preparation of relevant documents
- Supply chain progress checking and systematic report

Advantages:

Heavy lift vessel
Semi-submersible vessels
SPB vessel
SPMT
Professional warehouse
Own trucks and barges
Global network

Field of Services:

Marine engineering, shipbuilding industry, energy, petrochemical, metallurgy, cement, oil and gas, road and bridge construction and other industries
