 Dry Dock Logistic Solutions (DLS)




InterMax can manage, coordinate and efficiently anticipate on all required logistic movements of variously originated purchased spares when your vessels will go into dry dock. While your vessels are already in dry dock "last minute" or delayed purchase orders can be routed quickly and efficiently to your docking vessel as well. InterMax closely monitors all your suppliers worldwide and offers you the most efficient solutions when it comes to cost and time saving, in general we always try to combine both priorities no matter what situation your vessel is in.

Dry Dock Locations / Countries:

Besides our own offices, our network covers all main countries and locations that are regularly used for dry docking. InterMax has its own office and facilities in China and can arrange smooth and swift customs clearances and deliveries direct to your vessel in all Chinese shipyards.

Besides China, InterMax offers equal reliable solutions for worldwide shipyards. Working closely with our integrated overseas (IMLA) partner, we control customs clearance procedures and can enter most worldwide shipyards without conflict.

Consulting our knowledge and services will result into an accurate tailor-made plan and operation for all logistic requirements related to your planned dry docking, giving you control and clear visibility of all costs involved.

InterMax offers a full range of dry docking and new building related services in China.
Besides taking care of all the import custom clearances, InterMax is also able to enter most of the Chinese shipyards and dockyards and deliver your spares direct to your vessel.
