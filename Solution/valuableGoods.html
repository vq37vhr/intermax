 Valuable Goods Logistics




To combine our long-time experiences in logistics industry, InterMax provides tailor-made, personalized and optimum solutions to our clients based on their needs.

Fine artwork is different from other typical cargoes. It requires personalized packaging based on the material and shape of the actual artwork. Also, it has rather complicated exporting procedures. We’ve successfully handled precious fine artworks for some famous artists and galleries and provided one-stop service.

Services included:

● Providing related exporting documents
● packaging for different form and specification of artwork
● safe and stabilized road transportation
● Monitoring customs declaration and export procedures by our professional personnel

We can provide the most professional solutions of international delivery of personal effects for the people such as overseas students, foreign teachers and companies in China, immigrants and workers aboard. The door-to-door service is designed to solve the matter of transportation of the personal belongings and baggage. With our successful experiences for years, we have received trust from our clients. With innovation attitude, we have dedicated in this line for providing high quality service in the most favorable price. “Safe, Professional and efficient” is the service target we promised to each client.
